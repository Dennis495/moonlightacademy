About us

Moonlight Academy is a Kenyan Roman catholic National School established in the year 1984,Located in Kiambu County along the Nairobi-Thika Highway six kilometers from Thika.Moonlight Academy is ranked among the Top schools nationwide in Kenya certificate of Secondary education and has many eminent alumni.

Motto

Educating all students to achieve today and Tommorrow in a Global Community and Economy.We are also Empowering and Inspiring all Students as life long learners

Vision

Our Vision at Moonlight academy is to Empower students to Acquire,demonstrate,articulate and value knowledge and skills that will support them

Mission

We will prepare all Students for College and life by providing a challenging curriculum that connects students lives and their future in a safe,Supportive and nurturing enviroment
